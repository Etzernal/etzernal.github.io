Brief description



NONAME is a robot that can be used an educational tool to targeting students from age 7 to 16. NONAME has fixed and standardized connections to ensure that it is safe for younger students. In addition, it is also scalable, allowing students from different age groups to use the same product.

This project was chosen to be funded by SUTD Entreprenurship Centre.





My contributions in this project:

In this project, my role was more geared towards electronics and software, in terms of Arduino and wiring up the circuit. I was also involved in the integration of the electronics and software. I dealt with ultrasonic sensors, and motors as well as custom capacitative touch sensors.
