ROBOCLASH 2019



ROBOCLASH 2019 is the first student-initiated robotics competition held in SUTD by the SUTD Robotics Club for SUTD students. We had a total of 35 participants which was quite a large group, considering that it was the very first competition that we were holding. We also secured sponsorship from both Lionsbot International and the EPD faculty in SUTD for materials as well as the prizes which included tablets and Nintendo Switches.


In the hosting of this competition, I was part of the planning committee, as the finance and logistics in-charge. I sourced and purchased materials for the field and the participants, as well as took care of the budgeting for ROBOCLASH
